Worksheet · 01

Part 1 — Write Your Story

Tell a 2-paragraph story about anything you choose.

It can be real or imaginary — just make it yours.

Paragraph 1 — Set the scene

Paragraph 2 — Build or conclude

Part 2 — Find Your Central Idea

Central Idea

What is the most important message or main point of your story?
Write it in one sentence.

Part 3 — Supporting Details

Which details from your story support that central idea?

Choose 4 details — a word, phrase, or sentence from your writing.
